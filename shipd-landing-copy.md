## FAQ

### Does Shipd change my code?

No. Shipd is read-only. It analyzes your repo and produces a plan.

### Does Shipd deploy my app?

No. Shipd helps you decide what to do and what is required before deployment.

### Which platforms does it compare?

Shipd starts with modern app platforms like Vercel, Railway, and Fly.io, with more platform support over time.

### Why not just ask an AI assistant?

Generic assistants do not reliably ground deployment advice in your actual repo, and platform-native tools are biased toward themselves. Shipd is built for neutral, repo-aware comparison.
